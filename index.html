What is therapy

Counselling provides an opportunity to explore personal difﬁculties in a conﬁdential and supportive environment. This may include expressing feelings that are painful, which many of us experience at certain times in our live. When this happens, it can be difﬁcult to stay positive and cope with everyday life. Counselling provides a safe and non-judgemental space to explore these feeling, identifying why you feel the way you do, and encouraging you to find your own solutions and move towards self-awareness and personal growth.

Who am I?

Personal and professional experience

H ello, my name is Rosie.
I am a qualified counsellor with a degree in Counselling and a CPCAB Level 5 Accredited Certificate in Counselling Children and Young People. Through the use of age-appropriate counselling, I aim to empower my clients to express their feelings and help them bring clarity to their experiences.

I am an integrative counsellor who leans on a person-centred approach, working collaboratively with my clients to use interventions based on what works best for them and the issues they bring to therapy. I specialise in working with adolescents and young people, and I enjoy using creativity in my work where clients find this helpful.
