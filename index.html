A static site has no dynamic content and does not process any data - the content will stay the same until updated. A static site doesn't have some of the following functionality: forms, user sign-in, shop/store, and payments. These sites are generally cheaper as they do not require backend technologies like form validation and databases.

For a standard website, it takes 4 weeks in total. This means 2 weeks for the initial build. You then have a week for feedback. I take another week to action your edits. This timeline increases for more complex, custom websites that fall out of my scope for a standard build. Another factor is availability - I work on one product at a time. Doing so ensures I deliver the highest quality project to you. Please get in touch for more details.
