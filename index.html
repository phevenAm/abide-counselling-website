What is therapy?

A brief introduction

Therapy provides an opportunity to explore personal difﬁculties in a conﬁdential and supportive environment. This may include painful feelings, negative thoughts, mental health issues, or distress from a difficult event. Talking therapies can help you to explore these feelings, identify why you feel the way you do, and give you the tools and support you need to move towards self-awareness and personal growth.

Counselling sessions are 50 minutes. Where appropriate or necessary we can agree a more suitable arrangement.

I work in an open-ended way with both long and short-term clients. That means that I am led by the client's needs in terms of how long they need (and want) to attend counselling for. We will agree to review, roughly every 6-8 sessions, to check progress and to make sure you are still benefiting from counselling.

However, it is also important that we agree a planned ending which we can set in the introductory session or later in our work.
